About
These articles present my own plans and considerations regarding several practical inventions that aim to solve real-world problems. A key consideration throughout my approach is the cost effective production of these machines and establishing your own backyard supply chains with machine automations.

Being a firm supporter of the concept of open hardware, I now present my ideas to the public for their perusal, and with the intention of ultimately turning these ideas into complete and tested open hardware designs/blueprints, to be licensed under the CERN open hardware license (strongly reciprical).



If anyone would like to support this goal, please see either the contact page on how to get in touch, or the support page for donation links.
